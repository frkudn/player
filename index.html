Features

Everything You Need

Powerful audio features wrapped in a beautiful, distraction-free interface.

🎵
Universal Audio
Play any local audio file — MP3, FLAC, AAC, OGG, WAV, AIFF, M4A and 50+ more formats supported out of the box.
🎚️
10-Band Equalizer
Professional EQ with bass boost, preamp, and preset support. Shape your sound exactly the way you want.
🎨
Dynamic Themes
Dark & Light modes with dynamic color extraction from album art. Your player adapts to your music.
📝
Synced Lyrics
Real-time time-synced lyrics displayed beautifully as your track plays. Never miss a word.
📚
Smart Library
Auto-scan and organize your collection by artist, album, genre. Full-text search and smart playlists included.
⚡
Native Performance
Hardware-accelerated playback built with Flutter for buttery smooth 60fps animations and instant response.
Interface

Beautiful by Design

Glassmorphic audio cards and a clean layout that gets out of your way.
